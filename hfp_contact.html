Contact Us

Hope For Paws is a 501c3 organization. Our aim is to find a home in Suffolk or Nassau County for our beloved rescue animals. If you would like more information about what we do and the animals we have, please contact us today by calling 1-516-796-1000. We will answer any questions or concerns you may have about our process and the animals we have for you. We have been servicing people across Long Island for years and proudly find great homes for all of the rescue animals at our shelter. We look forward to hearing from you !

Hours: Monday-Saturday 10am-5pm, Sunday closed
